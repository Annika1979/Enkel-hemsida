My life in a nutshell

About me

About me, well I guess this is I. I was born 42years ago in Sabbatsbergs Hospital as the youngest of five children, and the only girl. As the only girl I was offcourse spoilt, perhaps not by my parents, but by all of my brothers. My mother had hoped for a girl to do girly things with but instead she got a daughter that assisted her father and brothers with fixing the cars and assembling things in the garage. For the past 17 years I have worked within the telecom industry in various companies. At the moment I work as a Product Manager and also Project Manager. I live with my husband Ade, my dog Jackie and my 3 children David, 17, Tumelo, 11 and Stella, 3.

My Interests

I am very much into working with my hands and producing things. Hence programming is perfect for me. What I also do is sowing. I sow clothes for me and my daughter (the boys are not that interested in my clothes). Everything I sow is in ankara or similar fabrics. Travel is also high up on my agenda. I went on my first trip abroad when I was less than a year and since then it has continued. I studied in the US for a year when I was a teenager. After highschool I stayed with my uncle and aunty in their house in Rio for some months. I have worked in Spain when I was 15 and then I have also traveled alot in my jobs. During this corona time we do not travel that much but otherwise we try to take a trip to Nigeria at least once every other year to visit my husbands family.

Curiosa about me

I have held alot of jobs in my days. While studying I was working both as a croupier and as a car hostess showing off new car models to journalists. I have worked at McDonalds, in a reception, in customer service, as a tester, packing matches in a factory in Spain as well as assembling x-ray machines for Siemens. For the past 17years I have however worked within the telecom industry and believe I atleast have a grasp of that industry right now.
